About Vascar Australia

Vascor is a dynamic Australian Engineering Design company that specialised network, structural and electrical design , drafting and certification services.

We own our offshore design house and have on-shore engineers and drafters to service our clients with our local workforce .Our company was founded by Aussie Engineers in Melbourne with a vision to address the qualified resource gap in drafting and engineering space for Structural, Telco and Defence, Rail and Mining projects.
